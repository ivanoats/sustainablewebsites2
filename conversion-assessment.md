# Conversion Optimization Assessment of About Page copy

Current state: The page is optimized for credibility and education but not fully
optimized for conversion.

What's working:

Strong authority building (founder story, lululemon, WSG framework)
Environmental statistics create urgency Clear CTA at the end What could improve
conversion:

Value proposition clarity - Appears too late. Consider leading with the
problem/solution before the biography CTA specificity - "Get in touch to
discuss" is vague. What's the outcome? Consider: "Schedule a free sustainability
audit" or "Get a custom roadmap" Risk reduction - No mention of consultation
being free, low-commitment, or what to expect Benefit focus - Focuses on "what
we do" rather than "what you get" (cost savings, performance gains, competitive
advantage) Single CTA - High-converting pages often have 2-3 CTAs at strategic
points No urgency mechanism - No deadline, limited availability, or incentive to
act now Quick wins for conversion:

Add a benefit-focused subheader under the CTA ("See how much you could reduce
emissions and improve performance") Mention "free consultation" or
"no-obligation" if applicable Consider adding a mid-page CTA after the
statistics section All external links now open in new windows. The internal
contact link stays as-is to keep users on your site.onversion Optimization
